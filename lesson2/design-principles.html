White Space and Clean Design

White space gives the programer the ability to let their content breath and give the reader the feeling of space around the content on the webpage, so the reader doesn't have feelings of claustrophobia while navigating the webpage.

PARC: Alignment is a process used by web-designers to place content is related information order, by doing this the reader will be able to retain more information that the web-designer wants the reader to absorb.
